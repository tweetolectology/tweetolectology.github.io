As part of the 'Tweetolectology' project, we are pleased to announce that we will be hosting a workshop in Cambridge in March 2021, inviting submissions from all researchers working on problems of morphosyntactic variation and change, especially in understudied languages.

The call for papers is now closed. The submission deadline was 31 January 2020.
